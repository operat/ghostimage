GhostImage, oh yeah!

A small script that adds ghost images to websites. Written in plain JavaScript; no dependencies, small size, easy integration.

A customer of mine came up with the idea that it would be cool if some keywords could be linked to images that would be displayed to visitors as they moved their mouse over these words. Oh yeah, great idea, I said, locked me up in my room and tinkered the code. It was fun. Since images are hidden, waiting for trigger events, to eventually appear and hover above the contents, I called it GhostImage. Because sharing is fun too, I open sourced the code.
